# Notes
# - SC and HK .subset.woff2 are retained on disk for future per-locale font-family
#   selection but are not currently referenced in global.css. TC's unicode-range
#   (U+3400-4DBF + U+4E00-9FFF) covers the simplified and traditional Han
#   characters for all three regions at the MVP whitelist scale.
# - JP kanji codepoints in the whitelist (e.g. 三菱, 任天堂) fall inside the TC
#   face's unicode-range and will render from the TC subset glyphs because the
#   TC @font-face precedes the JP @font-face in global.css. CJK unification
#   means TC vs JP glyph shapes differ for some characters; for the current
#   10-char whitelist the forms coincide. Phase 6 follow-up: split JP into its
#   own font-family ("Noto Sans JP") and select via :lang(ja) when real
#   Japanese tickers ship.
